Food Ordering

Choose your favourite items from the menu and add them into the cart. Don't just explore the menu tag! Explore our daily special menu and add the meals you wish to eat to the cart! There are separate sections for every meal type in the Menu section

Order tokens provided

Once you have done placing the order get your token. Wait for the notfication. You will be notified once your order is almost done, so you can move to the canteen and collect your order from pick up line that will be reserved for you! Enjoy ordering efficiently!

Play games!

Are you already in the canteen? Well don't be bored while you await the delicious food. Once you pay the amount required, you can get to play games or solve riddles So what are you waiting for? Log in or Sign up if you haven't, and order all the food you want - all while doing it wherever you are!
